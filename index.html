About Me

Born in Saudi Arabia to two insane, American expats who decided to stick around for the Gulf War. Fun family photo of everyone in gas masks (my sister and I were ages 3 and 5, respectively). Moved back to New Mexico, where I had to explain to visiting classmates, that there is indeed a camel skull in my living room. For high school I got the unique experience of actually attending a military boarding school. After that it’s all even more downhill and not worth writing.

Not fun fact: Was visiting the French side of my family when the infamous Nov 2015 Paris terror attacks occurred. And yes, they live in Paris.

I love rock-climbing, reading, a good k-drama, and all things related to food and cats. Seriously, if you’re making pesto with a processor you’re doing it wrong. Get that mortar and pestle, your tastebuds deserve it.

I play guitar and piano badly but with gusto.
